# Nexus Smart Home Hub - Technical Documentation

## Overview
The Nexus Smart Home Hub is a central control unit designed to integrate various smart home devices into a unified ecosystem. It uses the "Harmony" protocol to communicate across different brands and device types.

## Installation
1. Connect the Nexus Hub to your router using the provided Ethernet cable.
2. Plug the power adapter into a wall outlet and the hub.
3. Wait for the status LED to turn solid blue.
4. Download the Nexus App on your smartphone and follow the on-screen instructions to pair the device.

## Supported Devices
- Smart Lights (standard Zigbee and Z-Wave)
- Smart Thermostats (Nest, Ecobee)
- Smart Locks (August, Schlage)
- Security Cameras (Nexus-Cam, Arlo)

## Harmony Protocol
The Harmony protocol is a proprietary low-latency mesh networking standard developed by Nexus. It ensures that device commands are executed within 50ms, even in large homes with over 100 connected devices.

## Troubleshooting
- **LED flashing red**: The hub is disconnected from the internet. Check your router and Ethernet connection.
- **Device not responding**: Ensure the device is within 30 feet of the hub or another Harmony-enabled device.
- **App cannot find hub**: Make sure your phone is on the same Wi-Fi network as the hub.

## Safety Information
- Do not expose the hub to water or extreme temperatures.
- Only use the provided power adapter.
- The hub contains no user-serviceable parts.
